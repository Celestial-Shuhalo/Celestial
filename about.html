About to Celestial!

Celestial is a small raiding guild on the Turalyon server that has been around since June 2005, but previously on the Shu'halo/Eitrigg server.

Celestial is run by Hekus who has been guild master since 2014. Hekus is aided in running the guild by Rigourminos.
