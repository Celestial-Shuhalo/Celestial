Meet the Officers

Hekus has been leading the guild since 2013 as Assisant Guild Master and took over as official Guild Master in 2016. He enjoys raiding and getting to know the people in the guild.

Guild Master Hekus
Rigourminos (Rig) has been with Celestial since 2015 and rose to higher ranks by showing that he loves the guild as much as Hekus. He also loves to tell you all about Lore and runs a Lore page on Facebook. Don't hestitate to ask him all about it, or check out the Lore blog he updates.
Lore Master Rigourminos

Biredis has been with the guild for a very long time and has always been the voice of reason, especially when we talk about guild events. Biredis (or Scotty as everyone calls him) enjoys setting up the events that aren't progression raids, wether it be old raids, PvP nights, Keystone runs, etc. you name it, he'll try to set it up.

Events Director Biredis  

Xarari has previously been not only a Raid Leader but also a Guild Master giving her an excellent history of personal knowledge and experience to draw from. All the raiders value and respect her and enjoy running her raids every week.

Raid Leader Xarari

Cwealm has been an active guild memeber and friends with Xarari for many years, he always looks for the best in the guild and looks ot make the guild the best it can be. 

Cwealm

Callyn got into raiding recently in the big scheme of the game, he has since loved to help the guild and his guildies with anything they need in and out of the game. He looks to liven up the chat with his special sense of humor and calm down any issue that should arise.

Callyn

Dryade has been a raider and good friend of both Xarari, Lostnova and Cwealm. Dryade is always eager to help someone out and works tirelessly to keep the guild supplied with mats.

Dryade

Lostnova is a long time raider and has a lot of experience leading teams and guilds. Lostnova keeps everyone on track and loves to help out.

Lostnova
